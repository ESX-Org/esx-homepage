What is ESX?

Welcome, to the most popular FiveM framework - ESX. For years RP developers choose ESX and create their unique and immersive worlds together with the help of ESX. The framework itself was initially created over three years ago by "GiZz", today he put together a strong team of developers with whom he is now working on his new projects. ESX 2.0 as well as ESX-NEXT. Two major projects which will further revolutionise the modern RP genre within FiveM. The aim of ESX is simple, provide the tools and help developers need to easily fulfil their artistic visions. Join us today and create your very own server with ESX!
